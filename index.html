What we do

Three engagements. Defined scope.

We design and run the work that structural transitions require — from setting the direction to operating it.

01

Strategic Advisory & Market Definition

We help leadership teams decide where to go and why — entry into new markets, repositioning of existing ones, definition of the next phase. Strategy that answers the operational question: can we actually run this?

Engagement length: 3 to 9 months
02

Operational Scale-Up

We rebuild operations when growth has outpaced the structure that produced it. Process design, systems consolidation (ERP, commercial stack), supply chain, and the cross-functional layer that nobody owns.

Engagement length: 3 to 12 months
03

Fractional Commercial Leadership

We step in as commercial leadership — country manager, head of expansion, head of operations — for the window where hiring a full-time executive is premature but absent direction is costly.

Engagement length: 6 to 24 months

How we work
